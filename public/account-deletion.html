Account Deletion Request

We respect your right to delete your Ember account and all associated data at any time.

How to Request Account Deletion

Email us at: feedback@emberstories.com

Subject: Account Deletion Request

Include: Your registered email address

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your account information (name, email, profile)
  • All photos and videos you uploaded to Ember
  • All stories you created (private and shared)
  • Your story candidates and analysis history
  • Your household membership data
  • All associated metadata and preferences

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 30 days and send you a confirmation email when complete.

Important Notes

  • Irreversible: Account deletion cannot be undone. All data will be permanently lost.
  • Household Stories: Stories you shared with your household will be removed from all household members.
  • Backup Policy: Automated backups may retain data for up to 30 days for disaster recovery purposes only.

Questions?

If you have questions about account deletion or data privacy, please contact us at feedback@emberstories.com.
